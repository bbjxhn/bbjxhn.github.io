Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers rich flavors and hearty ingredients into a comforting, baked masterpiece. This recipe combines tender sheets of pasta with a savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella cheese, all brought together with a perfectly seasoned tomato sauce. The layers are carefully built, then baked to golden perfection, creating a dish that's satisfying and full of flavor. Whether you're making it for a family dinner or a special occasion, this lasagna promises to be a crowd-pleaser, offering a delightful balance of textures and tastes in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons died basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, divided or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Make the meat sauce
  6. Make the meat sauce
